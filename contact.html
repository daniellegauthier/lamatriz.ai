Danielle Gauthier, Founder Story

Physical Magnetism Consulting began as an exploration of how attention, technology, and embodied awareness interact in everyday life. The workshops bring together gentle yoga, digital ethnography, mapping practices, and collage to help participants reflect on their media environments and emotional landscapes. The work draws inspiration from contemplative traditions, experimental art, and community learning spaces.
