Gross National Happiness & Work at La Matriz

A research-forward framework for humane, sovereign futures.

Gross National Happiness (GNH) offers more than a national metric — it is a philosophy of governance rooted in care, reciprocity, and the dignity of inner life. La Matriz adapts this tradition into a pluriversal, cyberfeminist, research-grade wellbeing framework designed for emotional AI, decentralized archives, and cross-cultural consulting.

We learn from Bhutan’s lineage of spiritual governance but express it within the realities of computational systems, design research, and the emergent needs of digital publics.

2. Work Philosophy

At La Matriz, work is not a commodity. It is a relationship with the world.

Our commitments:

  • Respect for the interior life (no extraction, no coercion, no surveillance).
  • Slow thinking — prioritizing quality, attention, and symbolic fidelity.
  • Boundary clarity — emotional labor is compensated; creative labor is credited.
  • Plural expertise — computational rigor meets mythic lineage and aesthetic intelligence.
  • Harm reduction — design for safety, minimize overstimulation, counteract burnout.

La Matriz operates with a researcher's integrity and an artist’s conscience: precise, accountable, and attuned to human complexity.

3. A GNH Lens for Emotional AI

Emotional data is among the most delicate forms of human expression. Applying GNH ensures that our AI models center:

  • context (no decontextualized labels),
  • consent (user-controlled access),
  • care (outputs shaped by kindness and clarity),
  • co-authorship (users shape meaning-making).

Instead of prediction, we cultivate reflection. Instead of classification, we cultivate connection. Instead of extraction, we cultivate reciprocity.

This is the ethical backbone of SoulCoin, the Oracle, and the developing emotional AI model.

4. Community Participation

La Matriz’s wellbeing model is participatory. Users, communities, and institutions co-create the “pluriversal” archive:

  • Anonymous contributions to emotional datasets
  • Reflective color sequencing
  • Interviews, co-design workshops, and field research
  • Cross-cultural emotional lexicons
  • Consent-driven knowledge exchange

Participation is always voluntary and revocable. Collective authorship strengthens not only the dataset, but the community around it.

5. The Future: Pluriversal Governance

GNH is a living philosophy for a world that requires new forms of wisdom. As emotional technology matures, we aim to build:

  • context-aware AI that respects cultural nuance,
  • community-run data trusts for emotional records,
  • sovereign blockchain protocols that prioritize human wellbeing,
  • research partnerships rooted in dignity and reciprocity.

We believe that emerging technologies should follow the oldest laws of the world: care, respect, and balance.
