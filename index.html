Physical Magnetism  ·  Linguistic Cartography

rasin. racine. raíz. root. return.

Sylvia Wynter identified it: the breadwinner makes himself the origin point of our spatial axis, but calls it neutral. What you perceive as need, as normalcy, as knowledge, is calibrated to his story, which is encoded in every institution, every interface, every design system that presumes to help you.

The media is the axis. The axis is a psyop. You already know your struggle. I share what that knowing looks like, mapped and mobilized, from gifts that don't center the breadwinner.

My tools are built against that system. Not in opposition, which keeps the breadwinner at the center as the thing being resisted. These tools reorient. They place your sensing, your story, at the origin. They are a cybernetic feedback system for people whose perspective has been treated as deviation.
